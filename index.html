Frequently Asked Questions

Are these items truly authentic?

Yes. We work directly with generational artisans in Rajasthan. Each box includes a certificate of authenticity and the story of the artisan who crafted your piece.

Will it fit my modern Sydney home decor?

Absolutely. We curate pieces that blend traditional Rajasthani craftsmanship with a sophisticated, contemporary aesthetic suitable for premium Australian interiors.

How does White Glove Delivery work?

Our local Sydney team handles every delivery. We don't just drop a box; we ensure your heritage piece arrives in perfect condition, hand-delivered to your door.
